Alvarez Pat | Producer & Mix Engineer

I am an Argentinian Producer and Mix Engineer based in London, United Kingdom.
Born in Buenos Aires, I started playing guitar when he was 5 years old. After several bands and recording experiences, I studied Recording and Mixing Engineering at Escuela Tecson, and started working in studios since then. Being in a musical background throughout my life has encouraged me to stay open to plenty of genres and styles, such as rock, indie, hip hop, trap, pop, funk, electronic, psychedelic and more, which taught me different approaches to communicate emotion through the same language which is music.
